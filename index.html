Friday · May 15
~2 hrs after landing

You land at 12:40 PM and need to be in Waco by 4 PM. That gives you about 1.5–2 hours to grab lunch and see a bit of Austin. Here are friendly options for your group!

South Congress Avenue (SoCo) 🌟
Neighborhood Stroll
Austin's most iconic stretch — quirky boutiques, boot shops, murals, and great food all in a walkable strip. Perfect for a quick taste of Austin's personality. Easy for all ages and very accessible.
Address S Congress Ave, Austin, TX 78704
📍 Navigate to South Congress
ATX Cocina 🌮
Restaurant
Upscale modern Mexican in downtown Austin — beautiful presentation, gluten-free menu, lively but not too loud. Reviewers rave about the carne asada and handmade tortillas. Note: opens at 4 PM Fridays — great for dinner if you return to Austin!
Address 110 San Antonio St #170, Austin, TX 78701
Hours Opens 4 PM Fri · Better for a quick SoCo bite at lunch
📍 Navigate to ATX Cocina
Upstairs at Caroline 🍽️
Family-Friendly · Games
A 4.9-star downtown gem with great food, rooftop views, and games like foosball and Jenga. Multiple reviewers mention it was perfect for families with teens — Carter would love it! Open from noon on Friday.

Chip & Joanna Gaines
The iconic home of Fixer Upper — beautifully landscaped grounds, the Magnolia Market, food trucks, and the famous silos. Saturday morning is the perfect window — open at 9 AM, just a short drive from your hotel before the 2:30 ceremony.

Hours Mon–Sat: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM · Closed Sundays
Rating ⭐ 4.6 · 2,975 reviews · Free admission
